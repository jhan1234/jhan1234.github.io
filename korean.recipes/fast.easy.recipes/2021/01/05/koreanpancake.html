Korean Style Pancake (부추부침개) Recipe

2021-01-05 17:38

Korean pork ribs are usually marinaded in a spicy sauce. We tried a non-spicy version and our toddler loved it! We were actually very surprised at how much he enjoyed chewing the meat off the ribs. This recipe takes a couple hours. Baking the ribs for a long time at a low heat makes the meat much more tender. The meat just falls off the bone! We use less sugar and soy sauce the the original recipe but the ribs were still very delicious!
