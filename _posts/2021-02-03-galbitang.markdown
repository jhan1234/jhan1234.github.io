{% highlight ruby %}
Ingredients
  3 cups dried seaweed
  4 cloves of garlic
  1 tablespoon soy sauce
  1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  1 teaspoon of salt
  1/4 lbs of beef (stew or steak meat)
{% endhighlight %}

{% highlight ruby %}
Directions:
  1. Soak meat in water for 1 hour (optional)
  2. Mince onion, pear, garlic, ginger and add in the sugar
     soy sauce and water
  3. Place the meat and marinade in the fridge for 8-24 hours
  4. Grill meat of stove, oven or grill!
{% endhighlight %}
