LA Galbi is probably the most well known Korean dish outside of Korea. It is a
sweet soy sauce marinade that even the most pickiest people will enjoy. There are
many recipes but the marinade mainly consists of sugar, soy sauce, garlic and
onion. The Korean pear is often added because the enyzmes help tenderize the
meat making it easier to chew! In our recipe we use less soy sauce and sugar,
but feel free to adjust the ratios to your and your family's liking!

{% highlight ruby %}
Ingredients:
  ~ 1 lbs beef short ribs cut into strips (easily found in Asian groceries)

  Marinade:
    1 sweet onion
    2 1 Korean pear or Fuji apple
    5 cloves garlic, peeled
    1 inch piece ginger
    1/2 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
    1/2 cup soy sauce
    1/2 cup water
    2 tablespoons dark sesame oil

{% endhighlight %}

{% highlight ruby %}
Directions:
  1. Soak meat in water for 1 hour (optional)
  2. Mince onion, pear, garlic, ginger and add in the sugar
     soy sauce and water
  3. Place the meat and marinade in the fridge for 8-24 hours
  4. Grill meat of stove, oven or grill!

{% endhighlight %}
